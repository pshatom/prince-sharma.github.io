About Me

I am Dr. Prince Sharma, Postdoctoral Fellow at the Hopkins Extreme Materials Institute, Johns Hopkins University, specializing in computational materials science. With over 25 publications in leading journals such as Nature Communications, Acta Materialia, Advanced Materials, and Carbon, I am dedicated to advancing materials research to address global challenges. As a computational materials scientist specializing in the design and development of advanced materials for extreme environments. I am deeply passionate about teaching and mentoring, thriving on scientific discussions that ignite curiosity. As a collaborative researcher, I work with teams at NASA Ames, Ames National Laboratory, and other leading institutions, driving interdisciplinary innovation. My enthusiasm for coding fuels my research, while my love for cooking mirrors my creative approach to problem-solving. In the spirit of materials, I find inspiration in the delicate balance of order and chaos.
